 Red Line ('L')

The Red Line provides 24-hour train service between Howard on the North Side and 95th/Dan Ryan on the South Side via downtown Chicago.

This page contains

  • Hours of Operation
    • Timetables
  • Customer Alerts for this route
  • Route Diagram and Guide (with stations and station timetables)

 

Hours of operation

 

Weekdays

Saturday

Sunday/Holiday
Between Howard and 95th/Dan Ryan (via downtown subway)
Howard to 95th

  Service at all times, every day
95th to Howard

 

  • During weekday rush periods, select Red Line trains operate between Howard and Ashland/63rd via the Green Line.
